## Objective
Verify that the Papyrus vitrine website maintains consistent branding across all pages and elements.

## Prerequisites
- No special prerequisites required.

## Steps
1. Go to the Papyrus Vitrine Website: [https://papyrus.hownee.com/](https://papyrus.hownee.com/)
	- _Screenshot needed: Homepage showing main branding elements (logo, colors, fonts)._ 
2. Review the fonts, colors, logos, images, icons, and mascot(s) on various pages (Home, About, Features, etc.).
	- _Screenshot needed: Example of branding on at least two different pages._
3. Compare branding elements for consistency (e.g., same logo, color palette, font usage, icon style).
	- _Video recommended: Record a walkthrough comparing branding across pages._
4. Note any inconsistencies or deviations from the brand guidelines.
	- _Screenshot needed: Example of any inconsistency found._

## Expected Results
- Consistent visual identity across all pages and elements.
- If inconsistencies are found, document them and capture screenshots or video.

---
**Tip:** If you encounter any issues, please document the error message and steps taken, and attach screenshots or a video for troubleshooting.
